## Who uses Tuteliq

Tuteliq is built for any platform where minors interact online:

- **Gaming platforms** — moderate in-game chat, voice comms, and user-generated content
- **Social apps** — detect grooming, bullying, and self-harm signals in DMs and feeds
- **Ed-tech** — ensure safe learning environments with real-time content filtering
- **Messaging apps** — analyze conversations for emotional distress and predatory behavior
