Tuteliq is an AI-powered child safety API that helps platforms detect harmful content, analyze emotional well-being, and maintain compliance with child protection regulations including KOSA and GDPR. Integrate real-time safety analysis into any application with a single API call.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Safety Detection" icon="shield-halved" href="/api-reference/safety">
    Detect bullying, grooming, and unsafe content in text with per-message risk scoring aligned to KOSA harm categories.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Emotional Analysis" icon="brain" href="/api-reference/analysis">
    Analyze emotional patterns across conversation histories to identify distress signals and behavioral shifts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Voice & Image" icon="microphone" href="/api-reference/analysis">
    Process audio recordings and images for safety analysis, including transcription, OCR, and content classification.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Batch & Webhooks" icon="arrows-spin" href="/api-reference/batch">
    Submit bulk analysis jobs and receive results asynchronously via webhook callbacks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="KOSA Compliance" icon="gavel" href="/api-reference/compliance">
    Built-in coverage for all nine Kids Online Safety Act harm categories with audit-ready reporting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GDPR Ready" icon="lock" href="/api-reference/compliance">
    Data minimization, retention controls, and consent management designed for processing children's data.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
