## Three tracks

### Parents & Caregivers

For parents, guardians, grandparents, teachers, and coaches.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Modules** | 6 |
| **Duration** | ~90 minutes |
| **Quiz** | 20 questions · 80% to pass |

**What you'll learn:**

- How to identify, prevent, and respond to online threats targeting children
- Grooming recognition, sextortion, cyberbullying, and practical safety tools
- Age-appropriate conversation frameworks for discussing online risks

<Info>
  *"302 million children are affected by online exploitation every year. You don't need to be a tech expert — you just need to know what to look for."*
</Info>

### Young People (10–16)

For young people who want to learn to spot manipulation and protect themselves online.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Modules** | 5 |
| **Duration** | ~60 minutes |
| **Quiz** | 15 questions · 75% to pass |

**What you'll learn:**

- How to spot manipulation and know it's never your fault
- Online tricks, sextortion, and healthy digital friendships
- What to do and exactly who to tell if something feels wrong

<Info>
  *"It's not your fault if someone tries to manipulate you online. This track gives you the power to spot the tricks and know exactly what to do."*
</Info>

### Companies & Platforms

For product managers, trust & safety teams, CTOs, and compliance officers.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Modules** | 6 |
| **Duration** | ~120 minutes |
| **Quiz** | 25 questions · 85% to pass |

**What you'll learn:**

- Regulatory obligations under KOSA, the EU Digital Services Act, and the Online Safety Act
- Safety by Design principles and implementation requirements
- How to build trust with users by proving you protect their youngest members

<Info>
  *"With KOSA, the Online Safety Act, and the EU Digital Services Act — compliance isn't optional. Build trust by proving you protect your youngest users."*
</Info>
